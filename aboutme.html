Something about me


From an early age I was very interested in computer programs and logic but I never had a chance to work in this field before. I have been working as a customer advisor but last year, in the middle of the pandemic, which caused the company I worked for to make me redundant, I realised that it was high time to change my career.


Although I have studied Information Systems in the past and even participated in small digital projects, I never had the confidence to pursue a career in IT. However, recently, I decided to dedicate some time to study and to brush up on the programming knowlegde because I really enjoy the idea of creating apps to solve issues in the real world.


I wanted to be part of a immersive bootcamp in Software Development. I wanted to get some comercial experience of which the employers are looking in candidates and Code Nation are engaged in creating employable talent.


I believe that with this kick start, I'll succeed as a digital professional.
